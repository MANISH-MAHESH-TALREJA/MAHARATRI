Mangal Aarti

Mangal Aarti is the first Aarti of the day, offered before sunrise between 4 am to 5 am every day to Radha Krishna, and all dieties.
Darshan Aarti

Once the Deities are ornamented,dressed and worshipped privately, they give Darshan to all the devotees so that people can offer prayers and worship. The chief priest offers Aarti with lamp, incense, chamara, and peacock fan.
Raj Bhoga Aarti

This particular Aarti lasts up to 20 minutes. During the Aarti, food is offered to all the Deities. The chief priest also offers aromatic incense, fresh flowers, chamara, and a peacock fan to the Deities.
Dhupa Aarti

This Dhupa Aarti lasts up to 10 minutes. The chief priest offers Aarti with aromatic incense, fresh flowers, chamara, and with a peacock fan as well. After that, bhog of fruits offered to the Deities.
Sandya Aarti

Sandhya Aarti is better known as Gaura Aarti. All the devotees sing GauraAarti and offer worship to Lord Chaitanya. This is one of the most magnificent and wonderful arti where people from all over the place come to praise the Lord .
Shyan Aarti

It is the final Aarti of the day. This Aarti ceremony is the last chance to see and worship the Deities.
